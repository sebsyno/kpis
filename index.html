📊 B2B IT KPI Mastery Blueprint

Full-funnel KPI library for B2B IT — split by Product (software / SaaS) vs Service (managed / professional IT services), with bad / good / incredible benchmarks (2025–2026 data).

Attraction & Acquisition

Cost Per Acquisition

CPA

Total cost to acquire a single paying customer. Measures financial efficiency of campaigns. Source: CRM + Looker Studio.

Read alongside CAC and LTV — a low CPA on customers who churn fast is a false win.

Customer Acquisition Cost

CAC

All sales + marketing overhead (ad spend, salaries, software) to win a customer over a period.

SegmentBadGoodIncredible
New CAC Ratio$2.82 : $1$2.00 : $1<$1.00 : $1 (expansion)
2025 median: $2.00 of S&M to acquire $1 of new ARR (up 14% YoY). Expansion CAC is ~half the cost of new.
